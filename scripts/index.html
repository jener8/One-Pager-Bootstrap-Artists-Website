BIO


My name is Campion, I am a Canadian that has been living in Berlin since 2006. Before moving to Berlin I studied Political Science and Law in Victoria, Canada. During law school I began practicing yoga in order to help with stress reduction and mental focus, and there began my love of yoga. Since 2005 I have been practicing Iyengar Yoga and have studied with many senior yoga teachers. These studies have taken me to different parts of the world including India. I teach classes in English but am also fluent in German.

Services

Classes are available to small groups with a maximum of four people. One-to-one teaching is also available. Classes are tailored to meet individual abilities and needs. Students of all levels are welcome. My teaching is influenced by the Iyengar style, which focuses on asanas emphasising precise alignment and utilises yoga aids I have built into my studio space.

FAQS

What do I wear?

Something comfortable, ideally shorts, bare feet.

Should I eat before class?

Do not eat a heavy meal for at least four hours before class.

How long is a class?

90 Minutes.

Do I have to be fit and/or flexible to do yoga?

No! Yoga is appropriate for any age and level of physical ability, however if there are special injuries or a pregnancy then please contact me for information.

Can I come to class during menstration?

Yes, but alternative poses will be given for inverted postures.

What languages do you offer?

Classes will be taught in English, although I have taught in German and can give clarifications in German where required.
