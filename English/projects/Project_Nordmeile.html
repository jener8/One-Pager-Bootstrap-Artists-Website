Nordmeile

Rasche Ripken Berlin, Exhibition 'Vom Rand zur Mitte' 2012.

Short circuited to a deconstructive practise Spellmann acts upon a transformative, drawing centred strategy. He rebuilds paper boxes into sharpened simplistic models of standardised warehouse architecture. They are fantasy-able as production and business as much as storage and sales places. With a few turns of the hand they change into a incomprehensible picture of a colourful, uniformed word, in which just one thing is missing: the product.
